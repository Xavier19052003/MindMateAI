About MindMate AI

MindMate AI is your personal wellness companion. Built with AI, it helps you track your mood, chat with a CBT-trained bot, practice guided meditations, and set self-care goals. Whether you're a student, remote worker, or just looking to improve your mental health, MindMate AI makes self-care simple, accessible, and empowering.

Features & Benefits

MindMate AI combines Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), AI-powered coaching, and guided self-care tools to support your mental wellness journey.

CBT-Powered Chatbot

Get instant support through conversations with an AI trained in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, helping you manage stress and reframe negative thoughts.

Mood Tracking

Track your daily moods and receive personalized insights that highlight patterns, helping you stay aware of your mental wellbeing.

Self-Care Guidance

Access tailored tips, routines, and wellness exercises designed to support your personal growth and everyday balance.

Talk to MindMate the AI Bot

Need someone to listen? MindMate is always here. Our AI-powered bot uses CBT techniques to offer supportive, judgment-free conversations whenever you need them. Whether you're feeling stressed, anxious, or just need a quick mental boost, MindMate is ready to help you feel better in the moment.
