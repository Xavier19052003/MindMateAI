Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you're in control. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no hidden fees or penalties. Once cancelled, you'll still have access to the platform until the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Absolutely! We offer a 7-day free trial so you can explore MindMate AI's features all the way from mood tracking to our CBT-powered chatbot and before committing to a paid plan.
Do you offer student discounts?
Yes, we do. Students can enjoy reduced pricing to make mental wellness tools more affordable and accessible. All you need is a valid student ID to qualify.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit and debit cards, as well as secure online payment options. Our checkout is encrypted to ensure your details remain safe.
How do I upgrade my plan?
Upgrading is simple. Log into your account, go to "Account Settings," and select the plan that best fits your needs. Your new features will be activated immediately.
